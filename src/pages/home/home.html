HOW IT WORKS

To start using this system you have to register in this Application. After registration you may loin to you account and start using the system. After login you have to create a plan (Lengo) first before deposit. You have to deposit according to you plans(lengo) and ofcourse you may have more than one plan in the one account. you can view your transaction history (deposit history) and also the balance which are arranged according to your plans that yuo created earlier. After deposit by that fixed time you can withdraw your money, like deposit, withdrawal is done plans wise. You withdraw money in a certain plan.
